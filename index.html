My name is Stefany Andrade and I’m a Senior Visual Designer & Illustrator based on Brazil (Porto Alegre/RS)

ABOUT ME

Working with visual design since 2017, I’m a senior designer with experience in a broad range of design areas (UI, Web Design, Illustration, editorial design and graphic design) and design leadership.

With a bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design, I dedicate my career in improving interfaces, developing more visual appealing materials and creating original illustrations.

WHAT I DO?

I believe that a good visual is essential for every, brand, project and business, therefore design is the tool to build better products and experiences, as also is the way to solve complex problems.
